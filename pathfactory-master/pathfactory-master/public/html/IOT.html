Internet Of Things IOT


The Internet of Things, or "IoT" for short, is about extending the power of the internet beyond computers and smartphones to a whole range of other things, processes, and environments. The Internet of Things is actually a pretty simple concept, it means taking all the things in the world and connecting them to the internet.Iot is the onliy type of techology that lets you help you out through a samrt solution.For instance let us consider minor this that you use daily but doesn't notice that they are a part of Iot like smartspeakers(alexa,google home), smart watches, e.t.c,.This is the time period where Iot is stepping out of it's box and making new and more innovative things that are nore comfortable and handy in our day to day life, like a gloves make payments through Nfc and socks that help you find it's other one and many more.The main goal of Iot is to make thing smart and let you have a ease in handeling tech as the most simple way.
